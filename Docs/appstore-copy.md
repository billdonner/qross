## Description (4000 chars max)

Qross is a grid-based trivia game that combines strategic path-planning with knowledge challenges. Navigate from one corner of a colorful board to the opposite corner by answering questions correctly — but choose your route wisely, because wrong answers block your path.

**Pick Your Corner**

Every game starts with a choice. Survey the board's topic colors, pick the corner surrounded by your strongest subjects, and commit. The diagonally opposite corner becomes your goal. Different starting corners mean different strategies.

**Strategic Movement**

Move to any of the 8 cells surrounding your position — up, down, left, right, or diagonal. Correct answers advance your position and extend your glowing path. Wrong answers burn the cell permanently and cost a life. Plan ahead to avoid dead ends.

**Three Variants**

- Face Up: See every question before you move. Pure strategy — plan the perfect route, then execute.
- Face Down: Only topic colors are visible. Route by your strengths, but questions are a surprise.
- Blind: No colors, no questions. Complete fog of war. Maximum difficulty.

**Flexible Difficulty**

Choose board sizes from 4x4 (beginner) to 8x8 (expert). Larger boards offer more routing options but require more correct answers. Select from 20+ topic categories — play to your strengths or challenge yourself with unfamiliar subjects.

**Hints When You Need Them**

Stuck on a critical-path question? Use Show Hint to reveal a clue (+1 score penalty) or Eliminate to remove a wrong answer (+2 penalty). Use them strategically — the penalty adds to your final score.

**Share Your Results**

After every game, share an emoji grid showing your path — just like Wordle. Compare routes with friends and see who found the optimal path.

**Game Center Leaderboards**

Compete with friends and the world. Lower scores are better — can you find the perfect diagonal?

**What's inside**
- Square grids from 4x4 to 8x8
- 20+ trivia categories with 9,000+ questions
- Three game variants: Face Up, Face Down, Blind
- 8-directional movement with strategic path planning
- Two hint types with score penalties
- Shareable emoji result grids
- Game Center leaderboards
- App Clip for instant play from shared links
- Dark mode support
- No ads, no subscriptions, no tracking
